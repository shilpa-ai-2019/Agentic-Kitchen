 # Agentic Kitchen
**Agentic SwayampakGhar(स्वयंपाकघर)** - Your own multicuisine culinary AI assistant. Eat Healthy, Stay Healthy!

[![Streamlit App](https://static.streamlit.io/badges/streamlit_badge_black_white.svg)](https://agentic-kitchen.streamlit.app/)

Our goal is to help you master meal prep with these tips for planning, cooking, and storing nutritious meals that save time and help you stay on track with your health goals. 🌱

🎥 **Demo** - https://youtu.be/7t2tSxECepM 

🛠️ **How does it work?** 🎛️ 🍲
1. Search for '**Food Dishes**' or '**Recipes from Ingredients**' or simply '**Just a few easy recipes**'
2. Enter your food **preferences** like '**Diet Type**', '**Cuisine**', '**Number of Serving'**', '**Meal Type**', '**Preferred Recipe Language**', '**Restrictions & Allergies**', '**nutritional limits**' etc.
3. Click '**Generate Recipes**' button to receive recommendations right away!

💡 **Technology behind Agentic SwayampakGhar**
1. **Streamlit**: Creates a clean and intuitive user interface.
2. **google-genai**: Google Gen AI Python SDK provides an interface for developers to integrate Google's generative models into their Python applications
